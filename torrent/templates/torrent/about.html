About Bitiso.org

Bitiso is a torrent tracker that index opensourcesoftware. We ensure that torrent we've created have at least one seed, so it shouldn't die or be orphan.

OpenPGP

The Bitiso Team cryptographically signs its downloads using the OpenPGP standard. You can use OpenPGP tools to verify that the files you download come from us. OpenPGP tools

  • or Mac: GPGTools GPG Suite
  • or Windows: Gpg4win
  • ulti-platform CLI: GnuPG
